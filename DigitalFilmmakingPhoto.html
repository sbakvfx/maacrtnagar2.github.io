DIGITAL FILMMAKING [DFM]

The Digital Filmmaking course is one of the most preferred short term courses, where students can learn the basics and core areas of filmmaking, like pre-production techniquess, linear editing, sound editing, and clay animation. A hands-on experience on software like Photoshop, Audition, Premiere, and After Effects make you an expert, You can work as a cinematographer, director, producer, and screenwriter.

Course Duration: 144 HRS

COURSE CONTENT

  1. Pre-production
  2. Concepts of filmmaking
  3. Clay animation
  4. Digital design
  5. Basics of compositing
  6. Sound Editing
  7. Concepts of non-linear editing

Software Covered:

  1. Adobe Photoshop
  2. Adobe After Effects
  3. Adobe Audition
  4. Adobe Premiere

DIGITAL PHOTOGRAPHY

A picture can tell an entire story! That's the power of photography. This revolutionary course provides you with the ideal structure to learn and acquire the skills that help you perform exceptionally in the industry.




Course Duration: 40 HRS

COURSE CONTENT

  1. Principles of photography
  2. Working with ISO
  3. Exposure
  4. Concept of aperture and shutter speed
  5. Auto and manual focus
  6. Framing and composition
  7. Depth of field
  8. Outdoor photography
  9. Portrait and product photography
  10. Fundamentals of photo editing
  11. Studio lighting techniques

Career Options:

  1. Fashion photography
  2. Still life photography
  3. Wedding photography
  4. Wildlife photography
  5. Product photography
  6. Photo journalism

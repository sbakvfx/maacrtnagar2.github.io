BLENDER PRO

Blender is a free and open source 3D creative suite. It supports the entirety of the 3D pipeline - modelling, rigging, animation, simulation, rendering, compositing and motion tracking, even video editing and game creation. This software is catching up big time, where major mid-size studios have changed their 3D pipeline to the Blender software. This software is also being used to create 3D game assets.

Course Duration: 144 HRS

Course Content:

  1. 3D Design & Modellling
  2. Digital Sculpting
  3. Texturing
  4. Look Development & Rendering
  5. CG Lighting
  6. Particles & Dynamics
  7. Hair
  8. Fur
  9. Cloth
  10. Character Setup & Skinning
  11. Character Animation
  12. Compositing and Editing

Software Covered:

  1. Blender

CINEMA 4D PRO

Known throughout the VFX and Graphic Design industry as a leading motion graphics powerhouse, Cinema 4D is packed with all the tools you need to make engageing and professional graphic content. This course covers Cinema 4D's robust motion graphics, also known as MoGraph, effective lighting techniques and various tools and examples covering differenct aspects of broadcast design and 3D motion graphics. Aimed for both, the biginner, eager to jump into the 3D motion graphics world, as well as for the more seasoned broadcast designer who wants to deepen his knowledge of the features of this wonderful software.

Course Duration: 120 HRS

Course Content:

  1. 3D Designer
  2. Non-destructive Modelling
  3. Texturing and Look Development
  4. Lighting and Rendering
  5. Particle Dynamics
  6. Hair and Cloth FX
  7. MoGrag Tools
  8. Volume Baesed Modelling
  9. Rendering Solution with ProRender & Physical renderer

Software Covered:

  1. Blender
  2. Cinema 4D
